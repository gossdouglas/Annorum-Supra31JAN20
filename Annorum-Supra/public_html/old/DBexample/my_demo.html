    Woods Defeated Jones On Day 1 Of Match Play

    POSTED: 10:06 am CST February 26, 2009
    UPDATED: 11:01 am CST February 26, 2009

    MARANA, Ariz. -- Tiger Woods is preparing for his second round Thursday at the Accenture Match Play Championship.

    The world's No. 1 golfer returned to action Wednesday following an eight-month layoff and opened with a birdie and an eagle en route to a 3 and 2 victory over 64th-ranked Brendan Jones of Australia. It was Woods' first competitive match since undergoing reconstructive surgery on his left knee the week after his U.S. Open victory last June.

    Woods will be going up against Tim Clark on Thursday at Dove Mountain. The South African eliminated two-time U.S. Open winner Retief Goosen in Wednesday's opening round.
